About MetaCraft

Leading provider of innovative interior solutions in UAE.

Our Story

MetaCraft Interior Trading Company, based in the UAE, specializes in supplying high-quality interior and architectural finishing materials that meet international standards. With strong partnerships across the GCC and India, we offer products such as metal ceiling systems, raised access floors, partition grids, and decorative finishes for corporate offices, data centers, healthcare facilities, and industrial spaces.

Driven by quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction, MetaCraft combines design expertise with reliable sourcing to deliver sustainable and modern interior solutions. We welcome collaborations built on trust, growth, and shared success.

Our Mission

To elevate spaces with durable, fire-rated panels (e.g., 600x600mm RAF, Class 0/A1), swirl diffusers, and customizable interiors. Committed to financial planning, compliance, and client success across UAE free zones.
