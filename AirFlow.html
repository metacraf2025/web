Air Flow System

Provide superior cooling for managing heat loads in mission critical facilities. Available with top surface adjustable dampers. The steel perforated panels are same size and finish as the solid panel, so they are available with high pressure laminates, vinyl and rubber floor coverings interchangeable with wood-core panel/calcium sulphate panel.

Steel perforated panel is specially designed to allow air flow through the access floor up to the equipment.
