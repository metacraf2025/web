Panel Description

MetaCraft 800 lbf access floor panels (600 x 600 mm) are all‑steel welded units with an enclosed bottom pan and 144 fuse‑welded joints for high rigidity. The panels are epoxy coated (40–60 microns) and oven‑cured for excellent corrosion resistance. A lightweight, non‑combustible cementitious core is pressure‑injected to support at least 96% of the top surface, enabling the system to withstand an 800 lbf point load in accordance with CISCA test procedures. Locator holes at all four corners allow secure bolting to the understructure, while 16 concrete‑filled perimeter gussets enhance edge strength and overall system stability without affecting die‑cut trimming accuracy.

Salient Features of Die‑Cut Trimming

  1. Provides 60 additional resistance‑weld points, giving a total of 144 spot welds per hollow panel and making the composite panel significantly more rigid than earlier 64‑weld designs.
  2. Improves corrosion protection along panel edges compared to non–die‑cut panels.
  3. Ensures a consistent gap between adjacent installed panels, enhancing aesthetics (no carpet creases) and reducing air leakage when used in UFAD applications.
