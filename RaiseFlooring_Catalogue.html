Anti-Static Laminated False Flooring

The panel is made of cold rolled steel sheet, which is punched, spot‑welded and coated with epoxy powder. Inside of the panel is filled with foamed cement. The top steel sheet of the panel is laminated with fire‑proof High Pressure Laminate (HPL) or conductive PVC tiles. The borders of the panel are trimmed with antistatic PVC edges.

Advantages and Applications

  • Fast, easy and accurate installation.
  • Completely non‑combustible; Class A flame‑spread and smoke‑development rating.
  • Excellent rolling load and ultimate load performance.
  • Epoxy paint finish for lifetime protection.
  • Typically used in office areas, electrical rooms, server rooms, conference rooms, power plants and sugar factories.

Technical Details

1. Tiles

  • Size: 600mm × 600mm.
  • Thickness: 35mm / 33mm.
  • All‑steel construction with powder‑coated finish.
  • Cavity filled with cement slurry.

2. Pedestal

  • All‑steel construction, zinc‑plated.
  • Threaded bolt and nut for level adjustment.
  • Top plate size: 75mm × 75mm × 3mm.
  • Bottom plate size: 100mm × 100mm × 2mm.
  • Pipe thickness: 1.3mm.
  • Various heights available.
  • Pedestal pipe diameter: 25mm.

3. Stringer

  • All‑steel construction, zinc‑plated.
  • Size: 21mm × 31mm × 573mm.
  • Thickness: 0.8mm.
  • Shape: seamless rectangular section.
  • Hole distance: 550mm (center to center).
