Glass Partition

Glass partition systems come in various types like framed, frameless, and sliding, each offering distinct balances of privacy, light transmission, and functionality for offices, homes, and commercial spaces. Common materials include tempered, laminated, or acoustic glass with customizable finishes.

Frame System
Frame Partition

Framed glass partitions use slim aluminum or steel frames around tempered glass panels to provide structural support and a polished, professional look in offices and commercial spaces. They balance durability with aesthetics, offering more stability than frameless options while allowing light transmission.

  • Slim aluminum frames (20-50mm) for 12mm glass durability.
  • Modular panels with doors, seals for reconfiguration.
  • Frosting/tinting for privacy, demountable design.
  • More stable than frameless for UAE/India offices.
Frame System
Semi Frame Partition

Semi-framed glass partitions combine minimal framing‚Äîtypically at the top and bottom channels‚Äîwith large glass panels on the sides for a balanced, modern look that offers more support than fully frameless systems. They provide structural stability while maintaining high transparency and light flow in offices and commercial interiors.

  • Slim profiles (15-50mm) hold 8-12mm toughened glass; edge-to-edge sides.
  • Modular GP2510 (41.5x25mm) or GP18100 (18x100mm) channels for single/double/VIG glazing.
  • Sliding/swing doors, seals, powder-coat; 3m heights with brackets/clips.
Frameless System
Frameless Partition

Frameless glass partitions employ single large sheets of toughened or laminated glass secured by minimal top/bottom tracks or patch fittings, eliminating visible frames for a seamless, ultra-modern appearance. They maximize transparency and light flow in offices, homes, and retail spaces while providing privacy options via frosting or tinting.

  • 8-16mm glass (tempered/low-iron/LCD) up to 60" wide x 12ft high; curved options.
  • 1"x1"/1"x4" tracks in anodized/RAL; pivot/sliding doors, STC 47.
  • Patch fittings, guides, blinds for easy relocation.
